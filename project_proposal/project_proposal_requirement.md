**2. A Description of the Proposed Project**

- The UQ problem statement, motivation, and significance.
- What method(s) and implementation(s) you plan to use or develop.
- Contributions, plans, and timelines (for each team member).

**3. Format Guidelines**

- 2-3 pages (single-spaced, 11pt font, 1-inch margins).
